# Desks

A **desk** is an isolated workspace. Each desk has its own set of notes, tags, and folder on your filesystem.

Use desks to separate areas of your life or work, for example:

- A desk for personal journaling
- A desk for a work project
- A desk for research and reading notes

Each desk stores its notes in `~/Documents/Balise/{desk name}/`. The files are plain markdown, so they are portable and readable outside of Balise.

Switch between desks from the left sidebar. Create a new desk from the settings panel.
