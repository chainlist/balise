# Welcome to Balise

Balise is a markdown note-taking app that lives on your desktop. Your notes stay local, in plain `.md` files you can read and edit with any tool.

Here are a few things to get you started:

- **Create a note** with `Ctrl+N`
- **Search your notes** with `Ctrl+K`
- **Switch desks** from the sidebar

The notes in this folder will walk you through the main concepts. Feel free to delete them once you're comfortable.

Happy writing!
